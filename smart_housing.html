AI-based Smart Housing (2022-2024)

This project involves collecting various data from IoT devices to analyze occupant behavior patterns. Using AI models, we predict the real-time preferences of the occupants and develop an algorithm to automatically control the building's systems.

Project Details

  • Collecting diverse IoT data to derive occupant patterns and implementing a personalized window tint control service through AI model training.
  • Using smart window technology to regulate incoming solar radiation, improving occupant comfort and achieving energy savings.
  • Different occupants have varying preferred tint levels based on their behavior patterns and preferences.
  • Developing an algorithm to control smart windows by predicting the occupant's preferred tint level using a machine learning model.

Achievements

  • Developed and evaluated a machine learning-based model to predict smart window tint levels based on occupant behavior.
    (2023 Spring Architectural Society of Korea)
  • Research on daylight-based lighting control methods to ensure required illuminance and reduce lighting energy consumption during summer in clear sky conditions using smart windows.
    (2023 Fall Architectural Society of Korea)
