About Me

I first became interested in IT technology through the analysis of urban and transportation data, and during my master's program, I delved deeply into AI-based smart housing research. Specifically, I saw the potential to develop innovative technologies by integrating AI with architecture, urban development, and energy, which could improve people's quality of life. The research I conducted during my master's program involved preprocessing and analyzing data collected from IoT devices to derive AI-based control algorithms tailored to occupants. Through this experience, I developed a strong interest in finding practical and innovative solutions using AI and data analysis. Additionally, in a multidisciplinary project at graduate school, I developed a model capable of maintaining high accuracy while achieving fast processing speed for time-series data learning/processing. Moving forward, I want to integrate AI into various industries, such as urban development and energy, to drive technological innovations that can provide better quality of life. I aim to continue learning and challenging myself to become an engineer who leads the world toward a better future through technology. In the doctoral program, I plan to continue in-depth research. To achieve these goals, I aspire to contribute to the development of technologies applicable to various industries by advancing AI research.
